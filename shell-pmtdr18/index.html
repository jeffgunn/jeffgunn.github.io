Welcome

Get ready to celebrate this year’s PEOPLE Make the Difference REAL honorees in style at the luxurious Four Seasons Hotel Las Vegas in Las Vegas.

Shell has prepared an exciting program for all invitees including delicious cuisine, Vegas-style entertainment, an informative General Session including an inspiring guest speaker and Gala Dinner and Awards.  The excitement does not end there; you will have the afternoon of March 1st to explore and enjoy Las Vegas at your leisure.  After the Gala Dinner and Awards, join your fellow colleagues for an exclusive Shell After Party in the House of Blues Foundation Room located on the top floor of the Mandalay Bay Hotel. 

We have made all the arrangements to ensure your trip to Las Vegas is as memorable and worry-free as possible. We encourage you to explore each section of this website to find everything you need to prepare and register for your trip.

Registration deadline is Tuesday, January 30, 2018.

We look forward to seeing you in Vegas.
