General Information

Airport Check-in

When checking your luggage at your home airport, make sure your bags are checked through to McCarran International Las Vegas International Airport. The airport code is LAS.

Air Travel

Elevate IME, Shell's travel partner, will book airline tickets for you and your guest. Your program includes round-trip, economy class, nonrefundable airline tickets from your home city to McCarran International Las Vegas Airport.

For air ticketing, you must register using your names EXACTLY as they are shown on the government-issued ID you and your guest will present at the airport.  

All airline and seating requests are subject to availability. Within 48 hours of submitting your Registration form, an air agent will email you a proposed air itinerary for your approval. As airline tickets are only held for 24 hours, you must approve the proposed itinerary via email, or otherwise respond to the air department, within that timeframe to secure your schedule. Your air tickets cannot be booked without your written approval.

After your approval, you will receive an electronic airline ticket confirmation.  If you are extending your stay at The Four Seasons and have requested additional room night(s), Shell Events Headquarters at Elevate IME will notify you when these nights are confirmed.  Your air itinerary will be processed once this is confirmed.

Important information on fees:

  • Air bookings are based on the lowest cost fares available. Deviations from program travel dates or airline preference may incur additional air travel charges, which will be your responsibility.
  • After ticketing, any voluntary changes you make to your itinerary will incur additional charges at your expense. This includes corrections to the spelling or presentation of your name on your airline ticket.

Arrival at The Four Seasons Hotel

When you arrive at The Four Seasons Hotel, you will be escorted to the front desk for check-in. Once you have checked in, come to the Shell Hospitality Desk and we will provide you and your guest with welcome packets.
Check-in time is 4:00pm. If you arrive early and your room is not ready, you are welcome to store your luggage while you wait. The bell staff will store your luggage in a secure place until your room is available.

Attire

For meeting attendance, resort casual dress is required.

For other daytime activities, casual, comfortable clothing and walking shoes are strongly suggested. For evening activities, gentlemen might wear shirts with collars, and jackets as noted. Anything from simple classics to cocktail attire is suitable for ladies in dressier settings.

Welcome Dinner is resort casual

For the Recognition Dinner, gentlemen will need to bring a jacket and ties are optional.

Use these guidelines when deciding what to pack:

  • Casual: Dress shorts, slacks or jeans with dress t-shirts or polo shirts for gentlemen; sundresses, dress shorts, slacks or jeans with blouses or tops for ladies.
  • Resort casual: Slacks or khakis with collared knit or button down shirts for gentlemen; dresses, skirts or pants with blouses or tops for ladies.
  • Recognition Dinner: Coat/jacket and tie optional; Slacks with open-collared shirts and jacket for gentlemen, ties are optional; cocktail dresses or dressy pants ensembles for ladies.

Children

Children are not permitted to attend this program. Your guest must be at least 18 years of age. If you have questions, please direct them to your Shell Territory Manager.

Extended Hotel Travel

If you would like to extend your stay at The Four Seasons Hotel after the program dates of February 28 – March 2, 2018, the hotel may be able to offer the group rate of $239.00 + $25.00 resort fee + taxes per night, based on hotel availability. Please note that the hotel is currently sold out prior to February 28th. To guarantee an extension, please include your preferred dates and credit card information on your Registration form. Shell Events Headquarters, at Elevate IME, will confirm availability with the hotel. Your proposed air itinerary will be processed once this is confirmed.

Ground Transportation

Ground transportation between McCarran International Las Vegas Airport and The Four Seasons Hotel will be provided on the program dates of February 28 and March 2, 2018. Upon entering baggage claim you will be greeted with a “PMTDR” sign at the bottom of the escalator.  After you retrieve your luggage, you will be directed to your vehicle to the Four Seasons Hotel.

If you arrive or depart on any other date, you will be responsible for arranging and paying for your own transfers. Please note that taxis are readily available at the airport and hotel on a first-come, first-served basis. The cost is approximately $30.00 each way and will take about 15-20 minutes of travel time, depending on the time of arrival.

Hospitality Desk

The Shell Hospitality Desk will be located in the Palm Room. The Shell Travel Directors will be there throughout the program to answer questions you may have during your stay.

Internet Access

Complimentary high-speed Internet access is available in all guest rooms and public spaces.

Luggage

You may hand-carry one smaller bag that fits under the seat in front of you or in the overhead compartment.  Do not pack valuables, medication or travel documents in your checked luggage. Keep them with you. Keep prescription medicines in their original containers.

Due to the possibility your luggage is delayed, we suggest you pack a change of clothing and essential toiletries in your carry-on bag. All liquids/gels cannot exceed 3.0 ounces and must be in a clear quart-size bag. In the event your luggage is lost, report this to the airline desk in baggage claim and notify the Shell Travel Directors after you exit the airport.

Luggage Tags

Please use the “People Make The Difference Real” luggage tags that will be provided to you to identify your luggage. We ask that you make sure your name, email and phone number are printed on each one. It is also suggested that you pack an identification card inside each bag in case it is lost.

Time Zone

Las Vegas is in the Pacific Time Zone. When it is 12:00 Noon in Las Vegas, it is 3:00pm in New York, 2:00pm in Chicago and 1:00pm in Denver.

Tipping

Shell will prepay gratuities for airport porters, hotel bellmen, tour operators and wait staff for all hosted functions.

Valuables

We strongly urge you to leave valuables at home. However, a safe large enough for a camera, cell phone, and jewelry will be available in your guest room to secure any items you bring with you.

Weather

Las Vegas’ average high temperature in March is 73˚F and the average low is 46˚F. For up-to-date weather, go to www.weather.com.
