Las Vegas Dining Guide

Once known mostly for all-you-can-eat-buffets, Las Vegas has branched out as one of the top culinary destinations in the world. Featuring top chefs and restaurants such as:

  • Located more than 800 feet above Las Vegas at the Stratosphere Casino, Hotel & Tower, the award winning Top of the World Restaurant offers a dining experience like no other. This Las Vegas fine dining restaurant features culinary delights served in an elegant atmosphere with unparalleled views of the city as the restaurant revolves 360 degrees every 80 minutes
  • Mario Batali's Carnevino at The Palazzo features a 240-day dry-aged steak and modern Italian cuisine for an unforgettable meal.
  • Located within the Mandalay Bay Hotel, with its classic menu, inviting bar scene and sophisticated atmosphere, acclaimed Chef Michael Mina breaks new ground with his first steakhouse. STRIPSTEAK plays host to guests looking for the perfect filet in a modern alternative to the traditional steakhouse setting.
  • Wolfgang Puck is one of the most recognized names in the culinary world. And without going too far out of your way is the Wolfgang Puck Bar & Grill which is located downstairs in the MGM Grand.

Visit Open Table to make reservations during your visit to Las Vegas.
