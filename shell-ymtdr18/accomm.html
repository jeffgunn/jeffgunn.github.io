Check-In: 4:00 pm

On the iconic Las Vegas strip, luxurious Four Seasons Hotel Art-Deco inspired guest rooms reflect the energy and excitement of one of the world’s most vibrant cities. The Hotel offers direct access to the adjacent Casino and the city’s nightlife and entertainment.

Inspired by the glamorous art deco era, the hotel’s stylish guest rooms and luxury suites, echo the vibrancy and excitement of the world’s most iconic destination. Unexpected color pairings and bold, oversized artwork add interest, while sleek, high-quality furnishings and user-friendly technology ensure comfort. This Las Vegas luxury hotel is brilliantly positioned on the legendary Las Vegas Boulevard.

Hotel Amenities:

Four Seasons guests have access to two unique pool experiences – Four Seasons Hotels’ private pool, complete with signature passed amenities, and the expansive Four Seasons Hotel Beach at the adjacent Casino.

The Forbes Five Star Spa at Four Seasons' new, state-of-the-art Fitness Center features cutting-edge cardiovascular and strength-training equipment. A full range of the fitness industry’s most technologically advanced equipment by Matrix Fitness features virtual active technology, allowing guests to experience realistic journeys along iconic paths and through historic cities that turn each workout into an adventure. Views of the hotel’s lushly landscaped outdoor Fountain Terrace are visible through floor-to-ceiling windows.
