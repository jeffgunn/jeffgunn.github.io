Welcome

You Make the Difference REAL!

Legends of the Danube

Congratulations on your outstanding accomplishments in 2017 and on being named “The Best of the Best” at Shell. You have earned a spot amongst the elite and Shell would like to recognize these accomplishments with an incredible trip aboard your own private riverboat sailing the Danube River! This year, we invite you and a guest to a leisure cruise aboard the Avalon Panorama where you will experience the history rich cities of Budapest, Vienna and Salzburg.

Not only will you soak up the history and culture of some of the world’s most interesting ports, you will enjoy European river cruising, an experience as memorable as the destinations themselves. Throughout the six-day voyage, your ship, the Avalon Panorama will stay close to the shores of the beautiful Danube River, inviting you to sit back, relax and taken in the breathtaking scenes along the way. 

The Danube is the most international river in the world, touching 10 European countries as it flows from the Black Forest to the Black Sea. You will sail from Deggendorf, Germany to Budapest, Hungary, seeing grand cities and quaint villages lining the river, and exploring famous sites and hidden treasures ashore. Your rich itinerary offers authentic tastes of Central and Eastern Europe’s diverse cuisines, cultures, architecture and history.

We invite you to explore this website to learn more about your river cruise, ports of call, shore excursions and important travel information to help you prepare for your trip. The deadline to register is March 19, 2018.

Please note: winners and guests of You Make The Difference Real award trip depart the U.S. on May 2, 2018, and arrive in Munich, Germany, to embark the ship on May 3, 2018. At the end of the cruise, you will disembark and depart from Budapest, Hungary, on May 8, 2018, and arrive home the same day.

Your trip :

  • Airfare to and from your home city
  • Ground transportation between Munich Airport and Avalon Panorama on Thursday, May 3, 2018 and transportation between Avalon Panorama and Budapest International Airport on Tuesday, May 8, 2018
  • Accommodations for you and your guest for six days and five nights aboard Avalon Panorama
  • Specially selected optional and group activities
  • Exclusive dining experiences
  • Meals as shown on the agenda

Once again, congratulations and thank you for your dedication and passion for the Shell brand.  We look forward to hosting you in Europe aboard the Avalon Panorama!
