Welcome

You are invited to attend the 2019 Shell National Wholesale Conference, WINNING TOGETHER.  Join us to celebrate our combined successes and ongoing work under the Shell brand.  When we come together as One Team in 2019, we will set the foundation for continued progress and map out the challenges and opportunities that lay ahead within the ever-evolving business environment in which we operate. TOGETHER, we can WIN!

The conference will focus on 3 areas:

  • Winning Products and Services
  • Winning Experiences
  • Winning Partners

You will learn about new developments in these three areas, assess the competitive environment and explore how we can compete better through guest speakers, business seminars and the Experience Floor, both at a national level and more importantly, at a regional level – as we recognize that your main competition is at the local level.  We know that you will leave the conference with a full awareness of all the new offerings and even more assured that Shell is indeed your BRAND of CHOICE.

On the Experience Floor, you will have the opportunity to gets HANDS-ON with what Shell has to offer and you’ll get to see and hear from both Shell subject matter experts and select vendor partners.  We have engaged a select number of vendors to work with us to develop these experiences that aim for the heart of your business and how you engage the customer. You can expect to see several of the core vendors that support your business and meet a few new ones.

Our vendors will have their experts available to address all your questions, including any concerns, to ensure that you leave the conference fully informed. We believe you will be equipped to implement the marketing programs and technology enhancements in ways that meet the needs of your customers and drives increased business and loyalty for you.

We know you will be asking for more beyond the Experience Floor and will appreciate opportunities to network with our vendor experts and Shell staff through the informative seminars being offered. Following the seminars, you’ll have the chance to return to the Experience Floor and revisit the programs and have a chance to catch anything you may have missed the first time round.

The 2019 conference will include:

  • General Sessions
  • Business Seminars
  • The Experience Floor
  • Tuesday Evening Event
  • Wednesday Grand Finale Event
  • Networking Opportunities
