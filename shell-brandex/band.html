Lynyrd Skynyrd in Concert

The world’s busiest motion picture and television studio will host the Winning Together Closing Event in true Hollywood fashion. The scene -  an idyllic town complete with courthouse, town square, gazebo and tree-lined residential streets meandering off to a quaint neighborhood. Sound familiar? Well it should.  This backlot has been the set of countless Hollywood productions both on the big and small screen, from Rebel without a Cause and Batman to the Dukes of Hazzard and Gilmore Girls. This evening  Shell Town USA will come to life with fantastic food, studio tours, interactive entertainment and “special guests”. The evening culminates with Rock and Roll Hall of Fame legends Lynyrd Skynyrd taking the main stage on the steps of City Hall! The band is known for their Southern Rock-style and hits that continue to be a mainstay on classic rock stations. They continue to tour today bringing their blues-influenced gritty sound to a whole knew generation of fans.
