Activities

All activities are subject to availability based on minimum and maximum participation requirements.  This year, we have grouped activities according to type: land, sea and educational.

Four Seasons Resort Activities

Coral Gardens, Snorkeling and Sting Ray Adventure 

Explore the exquisite beauty and undersea life of Bora Bora’s lagoon during this stingray ballet and snorkeling safari. Depart the Four Seasons Resort and cruise by the southern tip of the Island, past Mt. Otemanu and Matira Beach. You will then drop anchor on a shallow sandbank to experience the elegant harmless eagle rays and bat rays. You will watch in amazement as your guide hand-feeds these gentle creatures, as they glide effortlessly around you. Next, you will continue for two additional stops for snorkeling and viewing at a pristine coral garden and outside the lagoon. Along the way, you’ll see an abundance of multi-colored, tropical fish, octopus, eel, and the more dramatic black tipped reef sharks.

Stingrays are known as the most graceful animals of the sea, these enormous creatures will glide with elegance past your boat again and again. Their fluid movement will make you think they are flying under water. You may be lucky to see a Manta-Ray if conditions and mother-nature are kind.

Deep Sea Fishing

The waters off the coast of Bora Bora in French Polynesia are rich in sea life and great for deep sea sport fishing. Fishing conditions are optimal in May, this is when the water is warmest. French Polynesia is one of the few places in the world where you can catch Pacific Blue Marlin all year round. Mahi-Mahi, Wahoo, Skipjack Tuna, Trevally, Jacks and Barracuda are also commonly fished during this time. The fishing grounds begin one mile outside the reef, where the water drops off to more than 1,000 meters (3,280 feet). You will fish aboard high-end vessels meant for fishing at sea, it’s designed and built exclusively for serious competitors, but novices don’t worry, you can learn just like the experts, all levels are welcome. There are typically 5 fishermen per boat.  Chef can prepare your catch.

Vaitape Shopping Experience

There is no better way to see and understand the Island of Bora Bora than via the Circle Island land excursion. You will depart from the resort by fast boat to Vaitape, the main island of Bora Bora. You will sit back and relax in your air-conditioned mini-coach with your English-speaking driver/guide, while seeing the sites of Vaitape in style. Along the way you will visit various souvenir and artist boutiques. You also pass by the sandy Matira Beach for a look over the vistas of the island. 

Tahitian Cooking Experience 

The Polynesian conviviality testifies of the “joie de vivre” in these islands, and this conviviality implies the art of the table. Polynesians are gourmets for whom everything deserves to be celebrated by a good “ma’a” (meal). Every event is an occasion to cook and to savor various dishes born from the diversity of the culinary cultures in our islands.  They believe great feasts to enjoy in family, with friends, or with your beloved one. Spend the afternoon with Four Seasons culinary chefs and learn some new techniques and enjoy the fruits of your labor.  Set alongside the beautiful scenery, you will enjoy a relaxed culinary experience.

The Private Naturalist Experience

Nestled within the grounds of the Resort is an inner lagoon, teeming with exotic marine life. The Ruahatu (God of the Ocean) Lagoon Sanctuary at Four Seasons Resort Bora Bora is more than just a spectacular snorkeling location, but also a research facility and a place where the marine environment not only thrives but grows, furthering the delicate and wondrous ecosystem. 

You will spend some time with Denis Schneider, the local marine biologist and expert of all things under the sea.  The sanctuary is home to over 100 colorful species, including octopus, eagle ray, lizardfish, Picasso trumpet fish, soldier fish, fire fish, unicorn fish, spotted puffer fish, parrotfish, peacock damselfish, clownfish and butterfly fish. Sea urchins, anemones and shrimp are also present, along with many other native species of marine life.  Guided snorkeling tours of the lagoon are part of today’s journey with Denis while learning all about the incredible marine environment and you will also learn about coral grafting. This is a truly special and unique opportunity to not only learn about their precious sanctuary but also contribute to it’s ever blossoming beauty.

Snuka

Discover a new way to explore the underwater world on a private Snuka excursion, ideal for couples. Allowing you to be underwater without carrying tanks and bulky diving equipment, Snuka is a cross between SCUBA and snorkeling. Your insructor will give a short briefing before diving alongside you as you take in the magical underwater world of Bora Bora.

WaveRunner Caravan, Circle Island

There is no better way to get up close and personal with the lagoons of Bora Bora than astride a Jet Ski. The Jet Ski caravan will take you on a journey of unparalleled beauty through the lagoons of Bora Bora's in a fun-filled new way during this scenic and exciting WaveRunner® excursion around the island. Depart from the resort’s beach where your guide will provide you with your safety and WaveRunner® instructions, then lead you on a journey to the lagoon's most remote and idyllic spots. En route, your guide will select the best location to stop for a swim. Stops may vary due to the wind and water conditions. Each WaveRunner® will accommodate two average-sized adults traveling tandem. A great way to see the lagoon.

Boat for Hire

Grab a friend or two and explore the island on your own with the guidance of a captain. Spend half the day charting your course to spend as you like, you may want to ask him to drop the anchor so you can jump in the water or stop in the town of Vaitape for shopping.  Whatever you decide, you are sure to enjoy taking in the beauty aboard on one of three boating options, The White Shark, Le Beacher or Champagne Boat.
