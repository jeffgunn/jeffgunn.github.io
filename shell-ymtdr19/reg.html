Registration

Air Travel

Elevate IME Shell's travel partner, will book airline tickets for you and your guest. Your program includes one-way, coach class, nonrefundable airline tickets from your home city to Franz Josef Strauss Munich Airport (MUC) in Munich, Germany and one-way, coach class, nonrefundable airline tickets from Budapest Rerenc Liszt International Airport (BUD).

After we receive your Registration Form, an air travel itinerary will be e-mailed to you. You must indicate your approval and e-mail your approval by the deadline shown on the itinerary before your reservations can be made. Seating requests will be forwarded to the airlines for assignment based on availability. We will do our best to accommodate your requests.

Once you approve your itinerary, you will be issued an electronic ticket and itinerary via e-mail. Approximately two weeks before departure, you will receive a travel package with special luggage tags, information on your ports of call and a program agenda. You will also receive another e-mail confirming your air itinerary just before the trip. 

Travel Documents

Every guest must have a passport valid for at least six months past the end of your trip. If you do not have a passport or yours expires before November 8, 2018, you must obtain one or renew it before the trip; otherwise you will not be permitted to board the airplane. You must register using your names EXACTLY as they are shown on the government-issued passport (and visa if necessary) you and your guest will present at the airport.  

All airline and seating requests are subject to availability. Within 48 hours of submitting your Registration form, an air agent will email you a proposed air itinerary for your approval. As airline tickets are only held for 24 hours, you must approve the proposed itinerary via email, or otherwise respond to the air department, within that time frame in order to secure your schedule. Your air tickets cannot be booked without your written approval.  After your approval, you will receive an electronic airline ticket confirmation,
