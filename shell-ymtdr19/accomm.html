Accommodations

Four Seasons Resort Bora Bora

Overwater bungalows and underwater adventures will be quite the experience at this sand-fringed idyll in the shadow of Bora Bora’s Mount Otemanu. Toast sunsets from your private plunge pool, marvel at the Tahitian night sky, or simply lose yourself in the lagoon, where the waters teem with exotic reef fish and colourful corals. 

The resort offers unparalleled service, and adventure by land and sea or just relax in your over the water bungalow on the sea.

Dining

Your many options on property allow you to dine according to your mood and preferences. Your dining choices include:

Arii Moana
Polynesian and French-infused dinner menu beneath the thatched roof at this romantic waterside restaurant.
Dinner Service Nightly from 6:00 pm – 9:30 pm

Fare Hoa Beach Bar and Grill
A casual toes-in-the-sand spot by the lagoon for all-day dining, nightly live music and tropical cocktails.
Lunch and Dinner daily from 11:00 am – 8:00 pm
Bar service daily from 11:00 am – 9:00 pm

Sunset Restaurant and Bar
An idyllic overwater setting for sunset cocktails paired with Asian-inspired bites and sushi.
Dinner service nightly from 5:00 pm – 11:00 pm

Tere Nui
Wake up each morning and dine in breathtaking views overlooking the lagoon and Mount Otemanu. Breakfast is served with a fresh sea breeze in this open-air restaurant.  A wide assortment of breakfast specialties include something for every palette, including detox juices. Tere Nui thought of everything.
Breakfast service daily from 6:30 am – 11:00 am

In-Bungalow Dining
Found only in Bora Bora, start your day with room service breakfast served in a ceremonial Polynesian canoe. In-room bungalow dining is available 24 hours a day and also includes options for a 20-minute menu and a to-go box, for those days where siting down for a meal is just not an option.
Breakfast service daily from 6:00 am – 11:30 am
All day dining daily from 11:30 am – 10:30 pm
Overnight dining daily from 10:30 pm – 6:00 am
